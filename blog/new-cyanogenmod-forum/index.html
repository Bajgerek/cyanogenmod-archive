New CyanogenMod Forum!

Ladies & Gentlemen,

I have implemented a new forum into the website. The purpose of the forum is for the disussions about the CyanogenMod products.

It was not built to compete with other forums, or act as just ‘another android forum‘.

IRC is great, but nothing is stored. People are often repeating themselves. We decided that a CyanogenMod forum would make life easier for everyone to search information and knowledge that will be stored in our threads. Think of this as another means of communication for the CyanogenMod products.

You can sign up here : http://forum.cyanogenmod.org (we also have a full mobile version of the forum if viewed from your device)

We hope you enjoy the new addition to the website.
