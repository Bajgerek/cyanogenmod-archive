February 19th, 2011 • Written by MrADeveci

New design thanks to IWM Network

The time has come to launch the new web site design.

The anticipated new design that won it’s spot as being the new face for CyanogenMod.org is finally here.

After working hard and closely with Stole Lazarevski of IWM Network the new web site is complete. Even after completion, we have both been working hard together to get the final tweeks and adjustments in place.

I would like to take this opportunity to publicly thank the guys at IWM Network for there hard work and ability to deliver as promised the new face for CyanogenMod.org and also recommend them for any web design work.

They will be working on a new ‘Mobile’ version of the site including a new design of our Forum so that comes into line with the new look.

We hope you like the new design as we steer towards a more community driven project, rather than one that is personal or single.

Feedback and suggestions are always welcome in our forums.

Thanks guys!

IWM Network : http://www.iwmnetwork.com
