Back by Popular Demand

Just in time for this year’s Big Android BBQ, the team at AndysThreads are doing another run of the CyanogenMod shirts we debuted last year, and they brought a new design with them as well! This re-print includes the Circle tee (blue or black) and the Circle hoodie we gave away as part of the BABBQ ’12 raffle. New this run  is a CM boot animation shirt (in black).

You can checkout the pictures and pre-order yours on the AndysThread website. Keep in mind this will be the final run of these items, so get them while you still can! The pre-order will end in 10 days, so time is running out!

We wanted to do something different this year as well, so CM’s proceeds from the sales will be donated to the EFF! Help us help out a worthy cause!

-The CyanogenMod team
